Divvy Project

This project looks at data from Chicago's Divvy system. Explore how weather affects the behavior of Northsiders vs. Southsiders. A few notes:

  • Data begins from 1/1/2014 to 6/27/2015
  • When entering queries please use single digits for months and days that are less than 10
  • Northside and Southside are divided by the lattitude of Madison Street
